Engine Displacement

Engine displacement is the total volume of all the cylinders in an engine. It is calculated using the formula:

Displacement (cc) = π × (Bore/2)² × Stroke × Cylinders

Where:

  • Bore: The diameter of the cylinder
  • Stroke: The distance the piston travels within the cylinder
  • Cylinders: The number of cylinders in the engine

This formula gives the displacement in cubic centimeters (cc), which is a standard unit for measuring engine size.

Note: Bore and Stroke are typically measured in millimeters, that is why there is a /10 in my calculation to convert from centimeters to millimeters

This value is crucial for understanding engine size and performance.
