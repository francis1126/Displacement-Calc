Camshaft Timing

Camshaft timing is crucial for engine performance. It determines when the intake and exhaust valves open and close, affecting airflow and combustion efficiency.

Key terms:

  • IVO: Intake Valve Opening
  • IVC: Intake Valve Closing
  • EVO: Exhaust Valve Opening
  • EVC: Exhaust Valve Closing
