Compression Ratio

The compression ratio is a measure of how much the air-fuel mixture is compressed in the engine cylinder before ignition. It is calculated as:

CR = (Swept Volume + Clearance Volume) / Clearance Volume

Where:

  • Swept Volume: The volume displaced by the piston as it moves from TDC to BDC.
  • Clearance Volume: The volume of the combustion chamber when the piston is at TDC.
